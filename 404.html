Everything you need

What is ElevenLabs and how does it work? +
ElevenLabs is an AI-powered voice synthesis platform that converts text into hyper-realistic speech. It uses deep learning models trained on thousands of hours of human voice data to produce audio that is virtually indistinguishable from a real human. You simply type or paste your text, choose a voice, and generate audio in seconds.
Is ElevenLabs free to use? +
Yes — ElevenLabs offers a generous free plan with 10,000 characters per month, access to all AI voices, and 3 custom voice clones. No credit card is required to start. Paid plans start at just $5/month for creators needing more volume and advanced features.
How realistic is the AI voice cloning? +
ElevenLabs voice cloning is considered the most realistic available today. With just 1 minute of clean audio, the AI can replicate a person's unique vocal characteristics — including tone, cadence, pitch, and accent — with extraordinary accuracy. In blind tests, users consistently rate ElevenLabs clones as indistinguishable from the original voice.
Which languages does ElevenLabs support? +
ElevenLabs supports 32 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Chinese (Mandarin), Korean, Hindi, Arabic, Russian, Dutch, Polish, Swedish, Turkish, and more. All languages are supported with native-level pronunciation quality — not robotic machine translation.
Can I use ElevenLabs audio commercially? +
Yes. All ElevenLabs plans — including the free tier — include a commercial use licence. You can use generated audio in YouTube videos, podcasts, ads, audiobooks, games, apps, and any commercial project. Enterprise clients have access to extended licensing and white-label options.
Does ElevenLabs have an API for developers? +
Yes. ElevenLabs offers a powerful, developer-friendly API available from the Starter plan and above. It supports real-time streaming synthesis with extremely low latency — ideal for building AI assistants, voice-enabled apps, interactive games, and any product that needs dynamic speech generation at scale.
